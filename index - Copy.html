Welcome

Hi, I'm Heain.
I'm passionate about numbers, visualizations, and insight that speak and empower people to do their best work.

I'm a data insights developer with experience in consulting and project management. My fields have been mostly in government or education, but I find myself curious about many different topics.

I've had some amazing opportunities to work with organizational leaders at the cross-section of data/information and project management. Through those experiences, I realized I really love the nitty-gritty of getting the data into understandable visuals. It's even more fun when it's interactive.

My hope for this platform is to showcase my project and share about my experiences as I focus on technical data analytics and visualization skills.

I've worked in many projects working with leadership to define key metrics, business insight strategies, and developing organizational systems to enhance data-driven decision making. Check them out at "about me".
